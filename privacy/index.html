Privacy

Privacy at a glance

Last updated: April 26, 2026

No ads, analytics, or tracking

Wocheneinkauf does not show ads, does not use analytics SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Data you create in the app

The app stores grocery libraries, catalog items, recipes, shopping selections, shopping runs, history, and your chosen display name on your device.

iCloud sync and sharing

If iCloud is enabled, app data can sync through Apple CloudKit using your iCloud account. Shared libraries are shared only with people you invite or accept through Apple's sharing system.

Support contact

If you email support, your email address, name if provided, and message content are used to answer your request.

Website hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical request data such as IP address and browser information to deliver the site securely.

Legal privacy notice

The full German privacy notice is available at wocheneinkauf.carullo.dev/datenschutz/.
