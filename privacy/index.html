Privacy Policy for Wocheneinkauf

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Summary

Wocheneinkauf does not collect analytics, advertising data, or tracking data. The app does not use a separate developer-operated server.

Data Stored By The App

Wocheneinkauf stores grocery libraries, catalog items, recipes, shopping selections, shopping runs, history, and your chosen display name on your device.

iCloud Sync And Sharing

If iCloud is enabled, app data can sync through Apple CloudKit using your iCloud account. Shared libraries are shared only with people you invite or accept through Apple's sharing system.

Tracking And Advertising

Wocheneinkauf does not track you across apps or websites, does not show ads, and does not use your data for advertising.

Apple Services

Apple may process iCloud, App Store, crash, diagnostic, and account data according to Apple's own privacy policies and your device settings.

Deleting Data

You can delete libraries inside the app where available, remove the app from your device, or manage iCloud app data in iOS Settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact git@carullo.dev.
